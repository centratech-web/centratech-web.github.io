Engineering Solidness & Innovation

Based on our 30 years long experience in environmental biotechnology, we develop novel technologies and bioprocesses for advanced biological wastewater treatment.

Our approach is based on upgrading the existing WWTP by introducing pre-selected microbial cultures in suspended form—such as flocs, aerobic granules, or cultures immobilized on activated carbon or curtains.

As a result, a higher process efficiency, better energy balance, and fewer investment and operational costs are consistently achieved across capacities ranging from 500 to 5000 Population Equivalents (PE).

Proprietary Solutions

SBR Technology

Compact modular plants designed to treat industrial and municipal wastewater primarily focusing on organic pollutants, biological, and chemical oxygen demand in compliance with strict EU regulatory standards.

Stages include: screening, extended aeration, sedimentation, and sludge recirculation.

ARGUS Systems

Aerobic Granular Biomass Upgrade System. A high-performance solution that combines biological degradation and solid-liquid separation in a single reactor.

Granular biomass is cultivated in a side-stream column bioreactor and injected directly into the main biological system, reducing footprint radically.
